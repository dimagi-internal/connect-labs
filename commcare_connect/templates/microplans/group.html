Is the control a fair counterfactual? The survey only visits the sampled settlements (PSUs), so the arms are compared on the selected PSUs, as a standardized mean difference (SMD). The verdict gates on settlement density — the settlement structure a control can't change after the survey. PSU size and building footprint are baseline covariates a difference-in-differences analysis adjusts for, so they are flagged to adjust at analysis, not used to reject the control. |SMD| < 0.1 is a close match, < 0.25 acceptable.
